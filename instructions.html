How to Play the Card Matching Game

Welcome to our Card Matching Game! Here's how you can play and have fun:

  • Objective: The goal is to match pairs of cards with the same value.
  • Game Setup: You will see a grid of cards face down. Click on a card to flip it over.
  • Playing: Flip two cards at a time to see if they match. If they do, they will remain face up. If not, they will flip back over. Remember what's on each card and where it is.
  • Winning: The game ends when all pairs are matched. Try to match all pairs with the fewest moves to achieve the highest score.
  • Scoring: You earn points for each match and lose points for mismatches.
